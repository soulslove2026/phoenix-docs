# Documentation Roadmap

## Milestone A — Foundation
Constitution, executive blueprint, product vision, market research, business model, roadmap, DDD, and enterprise architecture.

## Milestone B — Core Product Domains
Identity, social graph, voice rooms, messaging, communities, wallet, payments, gifts, notifications, and moderation.

## Milestone C — Engineering Bibles
Database, API, Flutter, backend, admin, security, DevOps, and testing.

## Milestone D — AI Platform
Moderation, recommendation, translation, fraud detection, support assistant, analytics, and forecasting.

## Milestone E — Ecosystem
Public API, SDK, plugins, mini-apps, developer portal, and marketplace.
