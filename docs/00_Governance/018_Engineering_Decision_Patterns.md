# GOV-019 — Engineering Decision Patterns

**Document ID:** GOV-019  
**Knowledge ID:** KN-GOV-019  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved

Preferred patterns include explicit ownership, bounded contexts, reversible changes, adapter-based integrations, append-oriented audit history, event-driven facts, progressive delivery, and graceful degradation.

Warning patterns include shared mutable state, provider SDKs inside business logic, hidden retries, direct balance edits, irreversible migrations, and undocumented exceptions.
