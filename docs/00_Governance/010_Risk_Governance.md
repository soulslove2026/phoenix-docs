# GOV-011 — Risk Governance

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Owner:** Risk Governance

## 1. Risk Categories

Phoenix tracks:

- product risk;
- technical risk;
- security risk;
- privacy risk;
- financial risk;
- legal and regulatory risk;
- AI risk;
- operational risk;
- provider risk;
- reputation risk;
- scaling risk.

## 2. Risk Record

Each material risk includes:

- identifier;
- description;
- owner;
- probability;
- impact;
- exposure;
- controls;
- residual risk;
- review date;
- trigger;
- contingency plan.

## 3. Risk Response

Possible responses:

- avoid;
- reduce;
- transfer;
- accept;
- monitor.

Accepted risk must be explicit and owned.

## 4. Critical Risk Rules

Critical risks require:

- written approval;
- compensating controls;
- monitoring;
- escalation path;
- expiry or reassessment date.

## 5. AI and Algorithm Risk

AI risks include:

- bias;
- model drift;
- unsafe recommendations;
- fraud evasion;
- privacy leakage;
- opaque enforcement;
- provider dependency.

## 6. Future Review Trigger

Review when Phoenix enters regulated markets or handles significant user funds.
