# GOV-010 — Founder Role

**Document ID:** GOV-010  
**Knowledge ID:** KN-GOV-010  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Owner:** Project Governance

## Mission

Preserve Phoenix's purpose, product vision, values, and strategic direction.

## Responsibilities

- define mission and market direction;
- prioritize capabilities;
- approve business model and monetization;
- protect user value;
- approve major risk decisions;
- ensure resources and timelines remain realistic;
- resolve strategic conflicts;
- preserve long-term ambition.

## Decision Rights

The Founder has final approval over:

- product strategy;
- brand direction;
- market entry;
- revenue model;
- investment priorities;
- organizational structure.

## Collaboration Duty

The Founder must seek architectural, security, data, legal, and financial review for high-impact decisions.

## Constraints

The role must not override critical safety, legal, or financial controls silently.

## Future Review Trigger

Review when governance expands to a board, investors obtain formal rights, or executive roles are delegated.
