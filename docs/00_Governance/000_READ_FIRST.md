## Governance Rule

No product, business, engineering, data, AI, security, or operational decision may override this governance layer silently.

Conflicts must be resolved through:

- documented analysis;
- named ownership;
- an ADR or governance amendment;
- impact and migration review;
- explicit approval.

## Phoenix Test

Every major decision must answer:

> Does this decision make Phoenix stronger in ten years?

If the answer is no, the decision must be rejected or redesigned.
