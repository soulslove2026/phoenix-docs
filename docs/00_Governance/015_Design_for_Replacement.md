# GOV-016 — Design for Replacement

**Document ID:** GOV-016  
**Knowledge ID:** KN-GOV-016  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Ratified

Every critical provider, framework, and service must be replaceable through Phoenix-owned contracts, adapters, data portability, and documented migration paths.

## Replaceability Test

Evaluate contract isolation, data portability, operational portability, feature parity risk, migration complexity, commercial dependence, and legal dependence.
