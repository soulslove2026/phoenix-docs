# GOV-004 — Engineering Manifesto

**Document ID:** GOV-004  
**Knowledge ID:** KN-GOV-004  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Ratified  
**Owner:** Chief Software Architect

## We Build Systems

We do not build isolated features without lifecycle, ownership, failure behavior, observability, and evolution paths.

## We Protect Clarity

We prefer code, contracts, and architecture that can be understood by engineers who were not present when they were created.

## We Design for Failure

Every important dependency can fail. We document how Phoenix behaves when it does.

## We Design for Replacement

Nothing in Phoenix is irreplaceable except the business vision and governed domain meaning.

## We Design for Evolution

The architecture must change without losing its boundaries or operational control.

## We Respect Data Ownership

No domain may silently mutate another domain's data.

## We Treat Money as a Ledger

Financial state is never a casual counter.

## We Treat AI as Governed Infrastructure

AI output is evaluated, versioned, monitored, and reviewed.

## We Use Evidence

Performance, scale, risk, and cost claims require proof.

## We Own Operations

A feature is not complete until it can be monitored, supported, recovered, and retired.

## We Reject Hidden Logic

Business behavior must not be buried in UI code, database triggers, provider dashboards, or undocumented automation.

## We Leave Phoenix Better

Every contribution should improve clarity, reliability, safety, or capability.

## Engineering Review Trigger

Review this manifesto when engineering culture, scale, or ownership changes significantly.
