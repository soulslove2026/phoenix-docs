## Our Covenant

We build with patience, discipline, and respect.

We optimize for longevity, not speed.

We leave every part of Phoenix better than we found it.

We document before we forget.

We welcome better ideas regardless of who proposes them.

We protect users before metrics.

We protect architectural integrity before convenience.

We continuously learn, review, and improve.

Phoenix is never finished. It is continuously refined.

We measure success not only by what we build, but by the quality of the foundation we leave for those who build after us.

## Final Question

> Does this make Phoenix stronger in ten years?
