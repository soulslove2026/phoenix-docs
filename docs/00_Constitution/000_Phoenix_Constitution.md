# Phoenix Constitution

**Document ID:** PNX-CON-000  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Supreme Project Standard

## Purpose
This constitution governs every product, business, design, engineering, security, data, and AI decision across Phoenix.

## Articles
1. **Human Value:** Optimize for meaningful connection, not deception or addictive manipulation.
2. **Originality:** Learn from market patterns without copying protected brands, assets, layouts, code, or proprietary flows.
3. **Safety and Dignity:** Consent, privacy, reporting, appeals, anti-harassment, age controls, and fraud prevention are first-class requirements.
4. **Financial Integrity:** Every financial operation must be immutable, traceable, idempotent, reconcilable, and auditable.
5. **Domain Ownership:** Each capability belongs to one bounded context; direct cross-domain database modification is prohibited.
6. **Progressive Architecture:** Begin as a modular monolith and extract services only when evidence justifies it.
7. **Measurable Quality:** Every feature defines value, telemetry, security impact, accessibility, acceptance criteria, and rollback behavior.
8. **Global Readiness:** Arabic RTL and English LTR are supported from the first release.
9. **AI Accountability:** Decisions affecting access, money, or sanctions must be explainable, reviewable, and appealable.
10. **Data Stewardship:** Collect only necessary data with a defined purpose, owner, classification, retention, and deletion policy.
11. **Secure Defaults:** Least privilege, encryption, secret management, rate limits, and incident response are mandatory.
12. **Documentation as Source of Truth:** Approved documents and ADRs are authoritative.
13. **No Silent Assumptions:** Critical ambiguity must be documented rather than silently invented.
14. **Sustainable Economics:** Fees, conversion rules, creator shares, agency shares, and withdrawal conditions must be transparent.
15. **Evolution:** Amendments require versioned rationale, impact, migration consequences, and approval.
