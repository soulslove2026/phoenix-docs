# Product Roadmap and Lifecycle

**Document ID:** PNX-ROAD-005  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Baseline

## Phase 0 — Foundation
Identity, profiles, localization, observability, admin baseline, CI/CD, backups, and threat modeling.

## Phase 1 — Core Social MVP
Voice rooms, room chat, relationships, communities, search, recommendations, reporting, moderation, and notifications.

## Phase 2 — Digital Economy
Coins, gifts, purchase verification, ledger, creator eligibility, withdrawals, reconciliation, and fraud controls.

## Phase 3 — Intelligence
Moderation assistance, translation, semantic discovery, fraud scoring, support assistant, and recommendations.

## Phase 4 — Creator and Agency Economy
Dashboards, memberships, ticketed events, recruitment, goals, and revenue statements.

## Phase 5 — Globalization
Regional infrastructure, currencies, country feature flags, local policy enforcement, and localized support.

## Phase 6 — Ecosystem
Public APIs, SDK, plugins, mini-apps, developer portal, and marketplace.
