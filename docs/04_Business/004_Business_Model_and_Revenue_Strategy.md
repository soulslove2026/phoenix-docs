# Business Model and Revenue Strategy

**Document ID:** PNX-BIZ-004  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Baseline

## Principle
Communication remains broadly accessible. Users pay for recognition, premium identity, convenience, entertainment, exclusive access, digital goods, and creator support.

## Revenue Streams
Coin purchases, premium gifts, VIP subscriptions, creator memberships and ticketed events, agency tools, digital identity assets, AI premium features, sponsored communities and events, enterprise offerings, and future marketplace revenue.

## Currency Model
- **Coins:** purchased or promotional spending units; never withdrawable.
- **Earnings:** eligible creator proceeds after fees, taxes, reversals, holds, and agency shares.
- **Diamonds:** optional engagement/display unit whose legal treatment must be explicitly defined before implementation.

## Financial Rules
Server-side purchase verification, immutable ledger, idempotent callbacks, compensating entries for refunds, segregation of promotional balances, KYC where required, sanctions screening, risk review, and no direct balance editing.
