# PEH-013 — Documentation Review Playbook

**Document ID:** PEH-013  
**Knowledge ID:** KN-ENG-013  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Baseline

## Review Steps

1. verify purpose and scope;
2. confirm alignment with Constitution;
3. verify domain ownership;
4. check vocabulary;
5. identify contradictions;
6. validate requirements are testable;
7. inspect security, privacy, financial, and operational implications;
8. verify links and IDs;
9. record open questions;
10. approve, request changes, or reject.

## Approval Standard

A document is approved only when two independent implementers can reach compatible results from it.
