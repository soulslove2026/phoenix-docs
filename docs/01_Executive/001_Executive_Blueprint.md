# Executive Blueprint

**Document ID:** PNX-EXE-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved Foundation

## Vision
To become the world's most intelligent social ecosystem where people connect, create, belong, and grow.

## Mission
Build a safe, inclusive, AI-native platform that enables real-time communication, community leadership, digital identity, creator income, and global discovery.

## Strategic Position
Phoenix is a social operating layer composed of voice communities, messaging, communities and agencies, digital identity, creator economy, digital gifts, AI assistance, and future public APIs and mini-apps.

## Product Pillars
Identity, Community, Communication, Creation, Monetization, Intelligence, and Trust.

## North Star Metric
**Meaningful Social Interactions per Weekly Active User.**

## Executive Metrics
Retention, WAU/MAU, voice-room minutes, healthy community ratio, creator earnings, payer conversion, payment success, moderation outcomes, crash-free sessions, and availability.
